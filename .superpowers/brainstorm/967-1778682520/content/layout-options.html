A — Full marketing page

Hero → features → how it works → social proof → pricing → footer. Standard SaaS playbook. Works well for cold traffic from social or ads.

⚡ Minimal product-led

▬ 3 short feature bullets

B — Short + product-first

Show the output immediately — a real before/after listing optimisation in the hero. Less explanation, more proof. Works well if you're sharing to Etsy seller communities where people scroll fast.

C — Problem → solution narrative

Opens with the pain ("Writing listings is eating your evenings"), then reveals SellWise as the fix. More persuasive for sellers who don't yet know they need an SEO tool. Longer scroll.
